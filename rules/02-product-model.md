# 02. Product Model

CORK is not a school achievement app.

CORK is a public arena of claims and verdicts.

## Core formula

```
Claim → Proof → Verdict → Reputation
```

A user brings a claim.

The crowd gives a verdict.

Reputation emerges from claims, proof, votes, arguments, scouting, and challenges.

## Core principle

We judge the claim, not human worth.

CORK may be sharp, ironic, and provocative, but it must not become harassment infrastructure.

## Claim, not achievement

The main product entity is Claim.

A claim may be:

- self achievement
- other achievement
- fail
- flex
- discovery
- debate
- absurd
- organization/project claim

Legacy Achievement may remain as storage/backward compatibility, but the product model is Claim.

## Verdict belongs to the crowd

The author chooses claim type and context.

The author does not choose the verdict.

Do not expose author-selected legacy labels like:

- "На корону"
- "На клоуна"
- "Рассудите"

as public product meaning.

The verdict is communicated through:

- crown/clown reactions
- verdict bar
- comments/arguments
- rankings

## Moderation honesty

If a claim is pending, it is not on trial yet.

Use honest copy:

- "Отправить на проверку"
- "Заявка отправлена на проверку"
- "На проверке"

Do not use:

- "Вынесено на суд"

unless the claim is actually visible in the arena.

## What CORK must not become

CORK must not become:

- a boring school portfolio
- LinkedIn for students
- a motivational diary
- a generic achievement tracker
- a meme dump without structure
- a harassment platform
- an app where friends politely rate each other

## Desired energy

CORK should feel:

- sharp
- understandable
- memetic
- structured
- alive
- useful when needed
- absurd when appropriate
- honest enough that verdicts have weight
